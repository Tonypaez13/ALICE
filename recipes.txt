Recipe: Classic Pancakes
Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 1/4 cups milk
- 1 egg
- 3 tbsp melted butter
Instructions:
1. In a bowl, sift together dry ingredients.
2. Make a well in the center and pour in milk, egg and butter.
3. Mix until smooth. Heat a griddle over medium heat.
4. Pour batter onto griddle and cook until golden brown on both sides.

Recipe: Spaghetti Aglio e Olio
Ingredients:
- 1 lb spaghetti
- 6 cloves garlic, sliced
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt to taste
- Fresh parsley, chopped
Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic and pepper flakes.
3. Add cooked pasta to the pan, toss with parsley and serve.

Recipe: Scrambled Eggs
Ingredients:
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 cup milk
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tbsp butter
Instructions:
1. Beat eggs with milk, salt and pepper.
2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
3. Pour egg mixture into skillet and stir until set.

Recipe: Chicken Stir Fry
Ingredients:
- 2 chicken breasts, sliced
- 2 cups mixed vegetables
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp cornstarch
Instructions:
1. Marinate chicken with soy sauce and cornstarch.
2. Stir fry chicken until browned.
3. Add vegetables and cook until tender-crisp.

Recipe: Tomato Soup
Ingredients:
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 4 cups tomatoes, chopped
- 2 cups vegetable broth
- Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
2. Add tomatoes and broth, simmer for 20 minutes.
3. Blend until smooth, season and serve.


Recipe: Avocado Toast
Ingredients:
- 2 slices whole grain bread
- 1 ripe avocado
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Red pepper flakes (optional)
Instructions:
1. Toast the bread until golden brown.
2. Mash the avocado and spread it on the toast.
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.

Recipe: Banana Smoothie
Ingredients:
- 2 ripe bananas
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup yogurt
- 1 tbsp honey
- Ice cubes
Instructions:
1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
3. Serve immediately.

Recipe: Grilled Cheese Sandwich
Ingredients:
- 2 slices of bread
- 2 slices of cheddar cheese
- 1 tbsp butter
Instructions:
1. Butter the bread slices on one side.
2. Place cheese between unbuttered sides.
3. Grill in a pan until bread is golden and cheese melts.

Recipe: Greek Salad
Ingredients:
- 1 cucumber, diced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 1/2 red onion, sliced
- 1/4 cup olives
- Feta cheese, crumbled
- Olive oil and vinegar
Instructions:
1. Combine all vegetables in a bowl.
2. Add olives and feta.
3. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar, then toss to combine.

Recipe: Baked Salmon
Ingredients:
- 2 salmon fillets
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon, sliced
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh dill
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Place salmon on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil.
3. Top with lemon slices, salt, pepper, and dill.
4. Bake for 15–20 minutes until flaky.
